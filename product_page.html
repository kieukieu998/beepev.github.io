What Other Drivers Say

Average Rating: 4.4

"The car is very smooth when driving since there are no gears and I love that it makes virtually no noise"

Tamara Melbourne 2018 BMW i3
Purchased Jul 2018 Verified

I love my i3 and I am excited to share about how much I love that car. I love never having to worry about gas and being fully charged each morning. The car looks small from the outside but feels spacious and more like an SUV from the driver's perspective. The driver's seat is extremely comfortable and I feel that BMW thought of everything in terms of comfort and convenience when creating this car. The car is very smooth when driving since there are no gears and I love that it makes virtually no noise.

"I enjoy the interior, but it could use improvement in a few small areas"

Tamara Melbourne 2018 BMW i3
Purchased Jul 2018 Verified

The i3 is a PERFECT commuter car for people who drive under under 80 mi/day highway or under 100 mi/day city. The powertrain, exterior, and interior design are daringly futuristic, but the car exhibits tight handling, a good upright seating position, communicative road feel, and heavy steering that remind me of classic BMWs like the e28 and e30 much more than any other new BMW I've driven.

"I really enjoy the i3 and I hope BMW continues to manufactuer the vehicles"

Tamara Melbourne 2018 BMW i3
Purchased Jul 2018 Verified

I really enjoy the i3 and I hope BMW continues to manufactuer the vehicles. I would like to see greater battery capacity - up to 400 miles on a charge and thus compare with the Tesla. My only real complaint is about the wheels; I believe they are a little too small although I understand the fuel savings reason for it. The ride would be smoother with bigger tires. The only other thing is why isn't BMW advertising the i3? People stop to talk to me about my car all the time an one even knows BMW makes electric cars!
